About Us

Why choose us ?

If you would like to experiment with new room decorating ideas, then surely you are in the right place . From diyas, clay diyas, hanging diyas (LATKAN), floating diyas, diwali candles, rangoli, pooja thali, curtains to torans, shubh-labh, GOLDEN LEAF HANDICRAFTS is out to make your festive season brighter & beautiful..
